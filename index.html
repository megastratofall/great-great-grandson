THE GREAT-GREAT-GRANDSON

An ALMOST Medieval Fantasy, CHAPTER I

Venture into the beginning of the story from the viewpoint of Furia and Virginia in the past and of Dross and Serena in the present.

Characters

In this adventure, you'll be able to control several characters, but the main ones are FURIA, VIRGINIA, DROSS, and SERENA, at least the protagonists of this first chapter. You'll discover the other characters with whom you'll also interact as the plot unfolds. Here's a brief description of each one.

FURIA

Grumpy old man, enjoys a good glass of beer and a potato pie on winter days. Despite his character, he managed to cultivate friendships throughout his life, not because he sought them, yet he is always surrounded by friends and a loyal companion he rescued many years ago from the clutches of the forest.

VIRGINIA

She still maintains her innocence from childhood, some might say she's a bit naive, but she's loyal, cheerful, and has a strong curiosity for knowledge. She's been Furia's companion since the day he rescued her from the dangers of the forest.

DROSS

He's an influential content creator on YouTube, known for his channel DrossRotzank. He loves donuts, empanadas, and dark humor. He has a 'small' fish named Mussolini and shares an apartment with his friend Fernando and Fernando's two daughters, Serena and Moira.

SERENA

At 8 years old, despite her young age, she's a very mature girl. Throughout her short life, she has been surrounded by adults and is a faithful companion of her father and her little sister. She's cheerful, optimistic, and affectionate.
